Evoke m~  _  

If m is a name, then 'm'~ is equivalent to m . For example:
   m=: 2 3 4
   'm'~
2 3 4
      
   m=: +/
   'm'~ 2 3 5 7
17

   m=: /
   + 'm'~ 2 3 5 7
17
