~. Improved initial writing: 2004-01-06
last updated: 2004-10-26

The monad ~. is now faster and leaner (as are other members of the i. family of facilities). The following benchmarks demonstrate the improvement.

b =: 1e6   ?@$ 2
b6=: 1e6 6 ?@$ 2
c =: a.{~ 1e6  ?@$ #a.
c2=: a.{~ 1e6 2?@$ #a.
c6=: a.{~ 1e6 6?@$ #a.
u =: u: 1e6 ?@$ 65536
i5=: 1e6 ?@$ 1e5
i6=: 1e6 ?@$ 1e6
i9=: 1e6 ?@$ 1e9
d =: 0.01*1e6 ?@$ 1e6
bx=: ":&.>?1e6$2e5
