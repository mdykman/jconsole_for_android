i. Improved initial writing: 2004-04-16
last updated: 2004-11-02

The dyad i. family of facilities has been improved:

 x i. y  also i: ; for floating point or general integer list x
 x i.!.0 y  also i: ; for boxed x and y
 (i.<./) y  also i: or >. ; for integer and floating point list y
 m&i. y 
 -.&n y 
 e.&n y 
 x (e.i.1:) y  

The first improvement (i. and i: for floating point and integer vectors) is minor, under 15% in time. Other improvements are more substantial; benchmarks demonstrating that are available by following the links above.
