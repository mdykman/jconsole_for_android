Shape

The shape of a noun is the list of the count of atoms in each of its axes. The monad $ gives the shape of a noun.
   a =. 4 2 3 $ 'abcdef'
   $ a
4 2 3
   $ 4		NB. atom has 0 axes and its shape is empty
   $ i. 5
5
