{y forms a catalogue from the atoms of its argument, its shape being the chain of the shapes of the opened items of y . The common shape of the boxed results is $y . For example:
   { 'ht';'ao';'gtw'

|hag|hat|haw|
+---+---+---+
|hog|hot|how|
+---+---+---+
+---+---+---+
|tag|tat|taw|
+---+---+---+
|tog|tot|tow|
