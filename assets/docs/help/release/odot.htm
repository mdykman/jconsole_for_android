o. Changed initial writing: 2007-01-09
last updated: 2007-01-15

The dyad o. has changed so that the following expressions give results with an internal type of floating point:

 9 o. y     real part
10 o. y magnitude
11 o. y imaginary part
12 o. y angle

For example:

   9 o. 3j4 _17
3 _17
   (3!:0) 9 o. 3j4 _17
8

   1 9 o. 3j4 _17
3.85374j_27.0168 _17
   (3!:0) 1 9 o. 3j4 _17
16
